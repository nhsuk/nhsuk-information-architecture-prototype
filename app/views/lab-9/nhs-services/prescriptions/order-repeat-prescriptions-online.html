Prescriptions and Pharmacies

Order a repeat prescription online

If you are regularly prescribed the same medicine, you can save time by ordering each issue of your prescription online without having to contact your GP surgery.

You can use this service:

  • to order a repeat prescription
  • to check the status of a prescription you have ordered

You cannot use this service:

  • if you are under the age of 13
  • if your registered GP surgery is not in England
  • to order a one-off prescription
