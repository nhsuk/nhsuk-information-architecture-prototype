Health A to Z

Cancer

Cancer is where cells grow and reproduce uncontrollably in a specific part of the body. These cells can invade and destroy surrounding healthy tissue, including organs.

Symptoms of cervical cancer

Breast lumps

Breast cancer in men

Spotting signs of cancer

Early signs of cancer can include changes to how the body normally works, or unusual unexplained symptoms.

Types of cancer

1 in 2 people will develop some form of cancer during their lifetime. There are many different types.

Cancer treatments

Solid tumours can usually be removed by surgery. Chemotherapy and radiotherapy are two other common treatments.

Reducing the risk of cancer

Simple lifestyle changes can greatly reduce the risk of developing cancer.

Cancer screening

Cancer screening involves having tests to check if a person has cancer, or is at risk.
