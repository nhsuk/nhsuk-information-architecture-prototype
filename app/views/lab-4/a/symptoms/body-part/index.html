Symptoms by body part

  • Skin, hair and nails

    Any changes to the skin, including rashes, itching and swelling

  • Lungs and airways

    Symptoms that affect the airways and breathing, including coughing and snoring

  • Head, face and neck

    Symptoms you feel in your head, face and neck including dizziness, earache and neck pain

  • Eyes and eyesight

    Symptoms that affect your eyes and your eyesight, including double vision, red eye and watering eyes

  • Back and shoulders

    Symptoms that appear in the upper body, including shoulder pain and back pain

  • Chest and breasts

    Symptoms that appear in the chest and breats such as breast pain, heart palpiations and hiccups

  • Arms and hands

    Symptoms noticeable in the arms, icnluding swollen arms, finger pain, and shaking hands

  • Stomach and gastrointestinal

    Symptoms that affect digestion such as bloating, diarrhoea, feeling sick and heartburn

  • Bottom

    Symptoms that appear in bottom, such as anal pain and worms in humans

  • Vagina and periods

    Symptoms including ovulation pain and heavy periods

  • Penis and testicles

    Symptoms that affect the penis and testicles, such as bloos in semen and testicle lumps

  • Legs, feet and toes

    Symptoms that appear in the lower body such as toe pain, smelly feet and knee pain
