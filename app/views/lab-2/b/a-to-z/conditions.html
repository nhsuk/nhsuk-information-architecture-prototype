Conditions

  • Allergies

    Conditions that cause allergic reactions, such as hay-fever

  • Arthritis

    Joint conditions that can cause swelling, stiffness, pain

  • Autoimmune conditions

    When the body mistakenly attacks normal cells, including conditions such as lupus and inflammatory bowel disease (IBS)

  • Breathing and respiratory conditions

    Illnesses that cause problems with your lungs, such as asthma and pneumonia

  • Cancer

    When abnormal cells in the body grow uncontrollably such as lung cancer, brain cancer and bone cancer

  • Dementia

    Conditions that cause problems with the way a person thinks and remembers

  • Digestive health conditions

    Problems that impact the way your body digests food and creates waste, such as Crohn's disease

  • Infections

    When germs in your body can cause illness, such as septicaemia

  • Neurological conditions

    Problems with the brain, nerves and spinal cord such as motor neurone disease and epilepsy

  • Sexual health conditions

    An injury to any part of the body, such as a head injury, sprain, strain or broken bone
