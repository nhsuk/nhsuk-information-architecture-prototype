Search results for sepsis

Immediate action required: Call 999 or go to A&E now if:

You have noticed any of the symptoms of meningitis, such as:

  • a high temperature (fever)
  • being sick
  • a rash that does not fade when a glass is rolled over it
  • a stiff neck
  • a dislike of bright lights
  • drowsiness or unresponsiveness
  • seizures (fits)

Symptoms can appear in any order. You do not always get all of the symptoms.

It's important to trust your instincts and get medical help if you need it.
